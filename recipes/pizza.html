Pizza

For begginers

This pizza dough recipe is quick and merely involves mixing a few basic ingredients and patting the dough into the pan â€” no need to wait for the dough to rise with this approach.

Ingredients

  • 3 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 tablespoon white sugar
  • 1(.25 ounce) package active fry yeast
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup warm water (110 degrees F/45 degrees C)
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 375 degrees C (190 degrees C).
  2. Combine flour, sugar, yeast, and salt in a large bowl. Mix in warm water and oil until dough comes together.
  3. Pat and stretch dough onto a large pizza pan. Cover with desired toppings
  4. Bake in the preheated oven until crust is lightly browned, 20 to 25 minutes.
